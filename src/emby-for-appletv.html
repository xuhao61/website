Emby for Apple TV

Available NOW on your 4th Gen Apple TV

 

Access all* of your Emby media now from your Apple TV device.

Smart Home Screen

Find what you want quickly and easily with a smart home screen design as well as individualized displays for each media type. Or use the full search capabilities including voice dictation (requires tvOS 9.2).

Easy Browsing

Or browse your TV and Movie collections with ease with smart library landing pages and grid views as well as search integration.

See All the Details

A beautiful detail screen presentation allows you access to all the information for a particular item as well as cast, related content and suggestions.

Hear the Music

Emby for Apple TV knows music too with easy access to artists albums and playlists.

Picture Perfect

Even your photos will look more beautiful in the Emby for Apple TV interface.

 

* Live TV not yet supported
