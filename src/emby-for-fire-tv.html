Emby for Amazon Fire TV

What you want to watch – right up front

Designed with with typical usage in mind, you won’t have to go hunting through your library to find the content you are most likely wanting to watch right now. The home screen shows you the items you were watching but hadn’t finished, your latest un-watched movies, the next episode of all your current TV shows and what’s on Live TV right now.

Download

Emby for Fire TV is available in the Amazon App Store.

Fast, easy browsing

For those times you do want to peruse your library, Emby for Fire TV makes it a breeze. Providing several ways to traverse your content and rich search capability, you’ll find what you’re looking for in no time.

Rediscover your content

Emby for Fire TV also uses the power of the server to help you find movies to watch for the first time or over and over.

The next of a new generation

The new Emby for Fire TV app is another in our new generation of more intelligent apps for all your devices. Designed more like the web client and fantastic Windows 8.1 and Win Phone apps, the Emby for Fire TV app continues our commitment to make Emby the absolute best solution for your personal media collection.
